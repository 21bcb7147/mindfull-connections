Yoga for Mind-Body Harmony

Studies of the benefits of yoga are only beginning to accumulate and so the evidence is not overwhelming or conclusive at this point. One of the problems with the studies is that they are done with small numbers of subjects and so may not represent the general population, and many are conducted in India and published only in foreign medical journals, making it difficult to know what rigorous standards the journals place on the researchers. Online communities and social media networks can give you peer support. These sites can be a space to talk about how you're feeling and share support. Some of us find it easier to communicate online rather than in person or over the phone. Being online can also help us talk more honestly about how we're feeling. And it can help us connect with others, especially if we're going through a difficult time.
